
# LAPERLE - GROUP NORMS AND COLLABORATIVE

## 1. Purpose

Our goal is to foster a collaborative space where every participant feels respected,
informed, and motivated to engage.
We aim to build a culture of shared ownership and mutual support.

### 2. Meeting Protocol

Agenda Preparation: The Facilitator drafts and
distributes the agenda at least one day before the meeting.

**Timeliness**: Sessions begin and conclude as scheduled,
with consideration for all time zones.

**Readiness**: Participants are expected to review the agenda and
any relevant materials beforehand.
**Note-Taking**: The Scribe captures key points
and action items, which are shared within 24 hours.
**Topic Discipline**: Discussions remain focused; unrelated
items are logged for future follow-up.

### 3. Schedule & Deliverables

**Planned Meetings**:

Every Thursday : Review draft and assign tasks.
Every Saturday: Finalize deliverables and resolve pending issues.
Recording Lead: Louis Kervens will manage meeting recordings.

**Participation Goal**: Actively participate in every session

### 4. Communication Channels

Main Platform: WhatsApp (primary), Google Meet (Group meeting and workplace)
Video & Archives: Google Meet for live sessions; recordings will be shared.
Polls & Voting: Doodle will be used for scheduling and decision-making.

### 5. Decision-Making

Voting Process: Decisions are made by majority vote;
the Facilitator resolves any ties.
Weighted Input: For complex topics, weighted voting will be
used to reflect diverse perspectives.
Equity: Alternate sessions will be scheduled to accommodate those who miss meetings.

### 6. Roles & Responsibilities

Coordinator: Designs the agenda, leads the meeting, and maintains structure.
Secretary: Documents discussions, tracks follow-ups, and shares meeting notes.
Meeting Monitor : Keeps the meeting on schedule,
signals transitions, and promotes time efficiency.

### 7. Respect & Empathy

Acknowledge and respect, time commitments, and working styles.
Offer feedback that is constructive and focused on ideas, not individuals.
Encourage balanced participation by practicing “step up, step back.”

### 8. Accountability & Trust

Follow through on assigned tasks within agreed timelines.
Reach out early if support or clarification is needed.
Address concerns openly and work together to find fair solutions.

### 9. Continuous Improvement

Host monthly reflection sessions using the “What worked / What could improve” format.

Adjust team norms and workflows based on feedback and evolving needs.
